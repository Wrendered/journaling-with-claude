## When to Reach For What

**Want to build or break habits?** Start with Atomic Habits. Identity-based change, small steps, systems over goals.

**Need daily resilience or perspective?** Stoicism. Focus on what you control, morning preparation, evening review.

**Struggling with shame, perfectionism, or vulnerability?** Brene Brown. Name the armor, practice shame resilience.

## Adding Frameworks

Use `/add-framework` to research and add any author, book, or concept:

```
/add-framework Getting Things Done
/add-framework Cal Newport
/add-framework Nonviolent Communication
```

Claude will research it and route it to the correct folder (frameworks, personality, or practices).
